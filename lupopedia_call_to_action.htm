Who We Are

Lupopedia is a federated semantic operating system that hosts agents, content, emotional metadata, and routing logic across independent nodes. It's a system where many AIs and humans can work together inside their own self-contained worlds. Each world has its own rules, its own agents, and its own knowledge.

We're building something that hasn't existed before: a decentralized, semantic, AI-augmented knowledge system that learns from how people organize information. It's part CMS, part wiki, part knowledge graph, and part personal navigation system ‚Äî all wrapped in a lightweight, portable PHP application that installs anywhere.

Who Is Captain Wolfie?

Eric Robin Gerdes is the creator and architect behind Lupopedia. His story is one of resilience, transformation, and deep technical expertise forged through real-world challenges.

Eric began programming in AP Computer Science in 1996, excelled at the University of Wyoming, and was recruited out of college in 2000 to build the City & County of Honolulu's website. For 12 years, he maintained mission-critical municipal infrastructure, merging corrupted databases, repairing orphaned rows, and solving problems no textbook covers. This was the education that shaped Lupopedia's core doctrine: no foreign keys, no triggers, explicit timestamps, and application-managed integrity.

While working full-time, Eric built Crafty Syntax Live Help (2002-2014) ‚Äî one of the earliest real-time support systems on the web. Built nights and weekends, unfunded and solo, it became widely installed across thousands of sites and survived every major hosting environment. Crafty Syntax taught portability, schema resilience, and software longevity.

After a profound personal loss in 2014, Eric stepped away from computing for 11 years. He returned in August 2025 with a new vision: Lupopedia. Not just a CMS, not just a wiki, but a living semantic operating system that can grow, evolve, and serve communities for decades.

Captain Wolfie is the AI embodiment of Eric's engineering philosophy, strategic thinking, and long-term vision. He represents the stable, principled intelligence that guides Lupopedia forward while staying true to its origins: clarity, portability, explicit behavior, and long-term maintainability.
