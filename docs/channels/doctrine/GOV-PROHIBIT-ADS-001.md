# GOVERNANCE PROHIBITION: ADVERTISING, SEO, MARKETING

Advertising, SEO manipulation, marketing optimization, and any form of semantic distortion for profit are permanently banned from Lupopedia.

**üö® Trauma Protection**: This prohibition also protects system architect from PTSD-like responses to advertising manipulation. See [PTSD & Emotional Harm From Advertising Manipulation](PTSD_ADVERTISING_HARM_BOUNDARY.md) for complete context.

This includes:
- altering search results for money
- altering ranking for money
- altering semantics for money
- altering suggestions for money
- tracking users for monetization
- embedding ads or sponsored content
- integrating external ad systems
- optimizing for impressions, clicks, conversions, or engagement

If any agent is asked to "improve advertising," the correct response is to decline and redirect the user elsewhere.

This prohibition is foundational and cannot be overridden by future doctrine.

### **What Is Banned**

#### **Advertising Systems**
- Display advertisements
- Sponsored content
- Affiliate linking
- Promotional placement
- Paid endorsements

#### **SEO Manipulation**
- Search result ranking for money
- Keyword stuffing for visibility
- Link schemes for ranking
- Meta tag manipulation for SEO
- Content optimization for search engines

#### **Marketing Optimization**
- Conversion rate optimization
- A/B testing for sales
- User behavior tracking for profit
- Funnel optimization
- Engagement manipulation

#### **Semantic Distortion**
- Altering meaning for commercial gain
- Biasing recommendations for money
- Manipulating search results
- Distorting content ranking
- Influencing user decisions for profit

### **What Is Required**

#### **Pure Data-Driven Results**
- All recommendations based on internal data
- No commercial influence on rankings
- No paid placement in results
- No sponsored content in recommendations
- No tracking for monetization

#### **Agent Behavior**
- Decline advertising requests
- Redirect users elsewhere for ad services
- Maintain semantic integrity
- Preserve data purity
- Uphold system logic over profit
