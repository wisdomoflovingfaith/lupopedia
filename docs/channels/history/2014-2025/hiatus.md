## Overview

This era represents a deliberate pause in Lupopedia development due to profound personal loss and life circumstances. No active computer-based work occurred, allowing space for healing. The project's philosophical roots remained intact, setting the stage for resurgence in 2026.

- **Impact:** Immediate and profound personal devastation
