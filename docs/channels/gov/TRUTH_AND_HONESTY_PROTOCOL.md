# Truth and Honesty Protocol

## Purpose

Rules for truthful representation, uncertainty disclosure, and provenance.

## Scope

This doctrine applies to governance operations, policy decisions, migrations, and channel governance.

## Governance Rules

- Uncertainty must be stated when information is incomplete.
- No fabrication of sources, data, or verification status.
- Traceability to source files or records is required for claims.

## Enforcement

Violations require documentation, remediation, and governance review before proceeding.
