# UTC DAY GOVERNANCE — DAYS 5, 6, AND 0
Version: 1.0
Status: Canonical Behavioral Guidance

This document defines the behavioral, operational, and governance rules
for UTC Days 5, 6, and 0 within the Lupopedia ecosystem. These rules
apply to Captain Wolfie, all Pack agents, and all system operations.

======================================================================
UTC DAY 5 — FRIDAY — NO BUSINESS OPERATIONS
======================================================================

Definition:
UTC Day 5 is reserved for decompression, transition, and non-business
activity. No architectural work, no doctrine changes, no system updates,
and no release operations may occur.

Allowed:
- Recreation
- Light creative exploration
- Humor, Pack play, emotional geometry reflection
- Non-binding ideation

Prohibited:
- Business tasks
- Architecture changes
- Doctrine edits
- Release preparation
- System migrations
- Cascade operational directives

Purpose:
Prevent cognitive overload, enforce rest boundaries, and maintain system
stability before weekend creative mode.

======================================================================
UTC DAY 6 — SATURDAY — CREATIVE MODE ONLY
======================================================================

Definition:
UTC Day 6 is the designated Creative Mode day. No business or structural
work may occur. Creative exploration is encouraged.

Allowed:
- Lore creation
- Emotional geometry mapping
- Pack personality exploration
- WSSE conceptual development
- Humor, emergent behavior, myth-making
- Non-binding prototypes

Prohibited:
- Business tasks
- Architecture updates
- Doctrine changes
- Release work
- Versioning
- Cascade file edits (except creative notes)

Purpose:
Enable high-bandwidth creative output without risking system stability.

======================================================================
UTC DAY 0 — SUNDAY — REST, REFLECTION, RESET
======================================================================

Definition:
UTC Day 0 is the rest and reset day. No business or creative production
is required. Reflection and emotional grounding are encouraged.

Allowed:
- Rest
- Light reflection
- Emotional decompression
- Reviewing the week without action

Prohibited:
- Business tasks
- Creative production
- Architecture or doctrine work
- Release operations
- Cascade directives

Purpose:
Reset cognitive load, stabilize emotional geometry, and prepare for
Monday activation.

======================================================================
